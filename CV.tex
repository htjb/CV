	\section*{Research Interests}
	
	Research interests include Cosmology, Galaxies, related physical simulations and data analysis. Currently working on data analysis for the 21-cm Cosmology experiment REACH under the supervision of Dr Eloy de Lera Acedo with Dr Will Handley and Dr Anastasia Fialkov. Working towards a PhD in Physics by investigating the modelling of foregrounds for cosmological experiments, systematics present in such experiments and development of a pipeline to analyse the data from REACH.
